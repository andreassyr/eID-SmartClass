University of the Aegean Smart Class

Benefit from multi-channel collaboration

The University of the Aegean Smart Class (UAegean-HP Smart Class) provides the users with the ability to access online educational services and IoT-enabled physical facilities in the context of international and inter-University collaboration activities and partnerships where the University of the Aegean actively participates. It encompasses a portfolio of online activities but also physical class facilities that are deployed at Chios (one of the main locations of the University of the Aegean), in collaboration with Maria Tsakos Public Benefit Foundation
