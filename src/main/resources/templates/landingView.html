UAegean Smart Class

Benefit from multi-channel collaboration

The University of the Aegean Smart Class (UAegean-HP Smart Class) provides the users with the ability to access online educational services and IoT-enabled physical facilities in the context of international and inter-University collaboration activities and partnerships where the University of the Aegean actively participates. It encompasses a portfolio of online activities but also physical class facilities that are deployed at Chios (one of the main locations of the University of the Aegean), in collaboration with Maria Tsakos Public Benefit Foundation.

Smart Class Terms Of Use

Users of the UAegean-HP Smart Class Services should register, via eIDAS and/or UAegean authentication, before accessing these services and this registration should be confirmed by one of the Smart Class Coordinators. More generally, the services and materials provided through this web site and by accessing the back-end system of the UAegean Smart Class, are subject to the following terms of use:

  1. You agree to use the Services only to exchange communication messages and materials that are proper to a responsible academic behavior and related to the particular Service
  2. You agree not to transfer the access rights granted to you for the needs of the participation in UAegean-HP Smart Class Program, to any other person or to a third party
  3. As a condition of your use of the UAegean-HP Smart Class Services, you will not use these services for any purpose that is unlawful or not compatible with the academic ethics. You may not use the these Services in any manner that could damage, disable, overburden, or impair any software and hardware asset related to the Smart Class Program, the University of the Aegean and its partner organizations

Integration of the UAegean Smart Class Services with the eIDAS Network has been implemented by UAegean | i4m Lab, and funded by the European Commission (CEF Programme - Agreement No INEA/CEF/ICT/ A2015/1147836)
